The Balance of Night and Day

Equinox Prime is a Warframe of duality, shifting between night and day forms to control the battlefield with ease.

Abilities

  • Metamorphosis - Switch between night and day form.
  • Pacify & Provoke - Reduce enemy damage or boost allies.
  • Mend & Maim - Absorb and unleash damage.
