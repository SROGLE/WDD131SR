This site features two specialized builds for the Warframe Equinox: a Day Build focused on damage and aggression, and a Night Build focused on crowd control and healing.

About Equinox

Equinox is a powerful Warframe that is known for its versatility and ability to switch between Day and Night forms. In the Day Form, Equinox excels at dealing high damage and taking out enemies in style. In the Night Form, she transforms into a healing powerhouse, supporting her team with powerful crowd control and healing abilities. Whether you're a lone wolf or a team player, Equinox can be built to suit your playstyle.

Abilities

Equinox's abilities are unique in that they change based on her form. In Day Form, her abilities focus on offensive damage, while in Night Form, they are geared toward healing and support.

Lore

Equinox is a manifestation of balance. She embodies the changing of the times, the ebb and flow of the cycle. Whether it's day or night, Equinox brings harmony to any mission.
